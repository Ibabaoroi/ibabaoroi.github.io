Roi Ibabao

Roi Ibabao graduated from Middlesex University in London and has achieved a 2:1 in BA Design and Illustration. This helped him develop a creative skillset, work with creative teams, review feedback and achieve what is required in design briefs. Roi has chosen the Test Analyst path due to his interest in technology and already aligning skillsets known in quality assurance has encouraged him to develop his skills further and pursue a career as a Test Analyst.

Roi also has a passion for anything art related and in his free time likes to do traditional paintings on topics ranging from wildlife, nature and portraiture art. Roi is keen to work with a client as a Test Analyst to ensure applications and systems are functional for their desired purposes.
